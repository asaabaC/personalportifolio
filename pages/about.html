About me

I am Asaaba Shallot, a passionate and self-motivated software developer based in Kampala, Uganda. I hold a Diploma in Computer Science from WITU Jolly, completed to build a strong foundation in technology and programming. With over 3 years of hands-on experience, I specialize in full-stack web development, creating user-centric applications with Python Flask, React, PHP Laravel, and Flutter. Currently, I work with a forward-thinking tech organization, enhancing my skills in Angular and TypeScript while developing an engaging bingo game and collaborating on website projects to refine my expertise. I have contributed to impactful projects in education, community empowerment, and digital accessibility. Beyond coding, I mentor young girls and children from underprivileged backgrounds toward tech careers through workshops and one-on-one sessions. I thrive on teamwork, communication, and a faith-driven approach, aiming to build digital tools that serve both purpose and people with integrity.
As a self-taught professional, I am passionate about coding and exploring new technologies to drive innovation.
